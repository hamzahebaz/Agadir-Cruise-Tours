Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common queries regarding shore excursions, port pickup, bookings, and ship delays.

Yes, all of our tour packages include private passenger pickup and drop-off directly from the Agadir port gate. Your driver-guide will be waiting with a sign matching your booking name.

We monitor cruise schedules in real-time. If your ship is delayed, we adjust our tour departure automatically to fit. If your ship cannot dock due to bad weather or itinerary changes, you will receive a full 100% refund, no questions asked.

Our excursions are custom designed for cruise timetables. We guarantee to bring you back to the Agadir port gate at least 1.5 to 2 hours before your ship's scheduled all-aboard time. We have a 100% on-time return track record.
