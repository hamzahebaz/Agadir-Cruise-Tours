Private Tours vs. Cruise Ship Bus Tours

Ditch the crowded 50-passenger cruise buses. Discover why booking independently is the smartest way to see Agadir.

June 28, 2026 • Written by Samia Tours Guide • 4 min read

When your cruise ship docks in Agadir, the cruise line will aggressively market their own shore excursions. They'll tell you that booking through them is the only safe option. But is it really? Let's compare private excursions with cruise ship bus tours across several key factors.

1. Group Size and Vehicle Comfort

Cruise ship tours typically load passengers onto massive 50-seater coaches. This means waiting for everyone to board, slow bathroom stops, and a rigid schedule. In contrast, private shore excursions utilize clean, modern, air-conditioned 8-seater or 17-seater minibuses. You'll travel in a small group of your family/friends, allowing for quick boarding and more comfortable seats.

2. Significant Cost Savings

Cruise line tours are notoriously overpriced. Because they take a massive commission cut, a basic city highlights tour can cost upwards of €80 to €100 per person. Booking directly with a local operator like Samia Tours allows you to skip the cruise line middlemen. A private minibus tour with a personal guide frequently costs 40% to 60% less per person, especially if traveling as a family or small group.

3. Flexible & Personalized Itineraries

If you want to spend extra time taking photos, skip a tourist trap shop, or stop at a local cafe for traditional mint tea, you can easily do so on a private tour. Your driver-guide is there for you and can adjust the route on the fly. On a cruise coach, you are bound to a strict, minute-by-minute itinerary with zero flexibility.

4. Better Access to Unique Locations

Large coaches cannot navigate narrow mountain paths in Paradise Valley, park close to historic monuments, or access off-the-beaten-path Berber villages. Our private minibuses can easily navigate these terrains, getting you closer to the authentic sights of Morocco.

5. Guaranteed On-Time Return to Ship

The number one fear of booking independently is missing the ship. At Samia Tours, we specialize exclusively in shore excursions. We monitor your ship's docking schedule in real time and guarantee to bring you back to the port gate at least 2 hours before the final boarding call. We have a 100% on-time return record over ten years of operation.
