From Agadir Cruise: Desert Quad Adventure

4 Hours
4.8 (189 reviews)
Local Experience

Overview & Description

Experience the thrill of riding a powerful quad through the Moroccan desert and along the Atlantic coast. Your adventure begins with pickup from Agadir Cruise or your hotel. After a safety briefing and equipment fitting, embark on an exciting journey through diverse terrain.

Ride through desert tracks and sand dunes, enjoy stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, visit a Berber village and sip traditional mint tea. Explore off-road trails across hills and villages with expert guidance from friendly professional quad instructors.
