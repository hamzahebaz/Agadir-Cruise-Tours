Overview & Description

Begin your adventure with comfortable pickup from Agadir Cruise or your hotel. Ride through beautiful natural landscapes, including sand dunes, lush forests, and the scenic Souss River.

Spot wild flamingos and enjoy the peaceful surroundings as your professional guide leads you along the route. Capture memorable photos and experience traditional Moroccan transport in a calm and safe setting.

After the camel ride, relax with a refreshing Moroccan mint tea before returning to your hotel or cruise pick-up point.

Activity Highlights

  • Enjoy the charm and gentle pace of camel rides
  • Spot wild flamingos along the Souss River
  • Ride through Agadir's dunes during the golden hour
  • Immerse yourself in peaceful nature and views of the royal palace
  • Relax with traditional Moroccan mint tea after your ride
