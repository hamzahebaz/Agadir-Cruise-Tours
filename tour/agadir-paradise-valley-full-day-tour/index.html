Agadir Paradise Valley Full-Day Tour

9 Hours
4.8 (156 reviews)
Guaranteed Port Return

Overview & Description

Begin your adventure with hotel or cruise port pickup in Agadir. Relax as you travel through the fertile Souss Valley in an air-conditioned vehicle. Capture the unique sight of goats climbing argan trees along the way.

Arrive at Paradise Valley, a serene oasis with natural pools and lush greenery. Enjoy a guided walk along the valley, take plenty of photos, and soak in the natural beauty. Savor a delicious traditional Moroccan lunch in a local Kasbah with couscous or tajine and fresh seasonal fruits.

After lunch, explore the palm groves or take an optional donkey ride. Return to Agadir in the late afternoon, refreshed and enriched by the authentic Moroccan experience.
