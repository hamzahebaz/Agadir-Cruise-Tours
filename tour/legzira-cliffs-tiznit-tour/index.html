From Agadir Cruise: Legzira Cliffs & Silver City Tiznit Full-Day Tour

9 Hours
4.8 (178 reviews)
Guaranteed Port Return

Overview & Description

Your adventure begins with pickup from Agadir Cruise or your hotel. Enjoy a scenic drive along Morocco's spectacular coastal landscapes to the fortified city of Tiznit, known for its red walls, ancient kasbah, and talented silversmiths. Stroll through the souks and admire handmade silver jewelry.

Continue along the coast to the legendary Legzira Beach. Famous for its natural rock arches carved by the Atlantic waves, it's perfect for a leisurely walk, breathtaking photos, or simply soaking up the Moroccan coastal scenery.

At midday, savor a delicious traditional Moroccan lunch at a local restaurant with ocean or landscape views. After lunch, enjoy some free time before a comfortable return to Agadir.
