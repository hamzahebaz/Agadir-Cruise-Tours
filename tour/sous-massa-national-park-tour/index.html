Overview & Description

Start your morning with a comfortable hotel pickup in Agadir and a 30-minute scenic drive to Souss-Massa National Park. Covering over 33,800 hectares, this conservation area was established in 1991 to protect and reintroduce Saharan wildlife. Visit the park museum to learn about local fauna and conservation efforts. Spot animals such as Addax, Dama gazelles, ostriches, Dorcas gazelles, and the magnificent Scimitar-horned Oryx. The park also hosts over 250 bird species, including the endangered Bald Ibis.

Throughout the excursion, your expert guide will provide insights into the ecosystem, animal behavior, and Morocco's environmental initiatives. After enjoying the park's beauty and serenity, you will be transported back to your hotel in Agadir.
