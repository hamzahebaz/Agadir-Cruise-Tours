Overview & Description

Your adventure begins with pickup from Agadir Cruise or Taghazout hotel, where your friendly professional driver-guide welcomes you aboard a comfortable 4x4.

Visit a traditional pottery house to discover one of Morocco's oldest decorative arts. Continue to the pristine beaches to admire the Atlantic coastline and the famous Fishermen's Caves carved into the cliffs.

Explore Souss-Massa National Park, home to rare birds such as flamingos, glossy ibises, and marbled ducks.

Stop in Tiznit, Morocco's silver capital, stroll through its medina, admire handmade Berber jewelry, and learn about the town's rich history.

Enjoy a delicious Berber lunch in a local home with tajine, couscous, fresh fruits, and mint tea. After lunch, take a camel ride across the picturesque Mini-Sahara dunes of Rsmouka, perfect for photos and connecting with the desert landscape.

End the day at Youssef Ben Tachfine Dam for peaceful hilltop views before returning to Agadir Cruise or Taghazout.

Tour Highlights

  • Enjoy a camel ride across the Mini-Sahara dunes
  • Savor a traditional Berber lunch including tajine, couscous, fresh fruits, and mint tea
  • Explore Youssef Ben Tachfine Dam with breathtaking panoramic views
  • Visit a traditional pottery house and learn about ancient Moroccan ceramics
  • Stroll through Tiznit, Morocco's silver capital, and its historic medina
  • Discover the Fishermen's Caves and pristine, untouched beaches
  • Visit Souss-Massa National Park, home to rare birds and desert flora
