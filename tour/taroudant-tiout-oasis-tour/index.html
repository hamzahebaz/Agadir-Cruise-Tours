From Agadir Cruise: Full-Day Tour to Taroudant & Tiout Oasis with Lunch

9 Hours
4.8 (167 reviews)
Guaranteed Port Return

Overview & Description

Your adventure begins with pickup from Agadir Cruise or your hotel. Relax as you travel through the fertile Souss Valley in a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle. Stop along the way to take photos of the unique goats climbing argan trees.

Arrive in Taroudant, a historic walled city with red clay ramparts and views of the Atlas Mountains. Follow your guide through the traditional souk, where artisans sell pottery, jewelry, rugs, and more. Walk along the city walls for panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.

Continue to the palm-filled oasis of Tiout, where you'll visit a picturesque Kasbah atop a hill, featured in the classic film 'Ali Baba and the 40 Thieves.' Enjoy a homemade Moroccan lunch of couscous or tajine with seasonal fruits. Take a peaceful walk through the oasis or opt for an optional donkey ride through the palm groves.

After experiencing the natural beauty and local hospitality, return to Agadir in the late afternoon, refreshed and enriched by authentic Moroccan culture.
