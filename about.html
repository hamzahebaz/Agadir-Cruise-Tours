Who We Are

Dedicated Shore Excursions Specialists

Samia Tours (licensed tourist transport company) operates Agadir Cruise Tours from our office located at N° 76 Chemin des dunes, Agadir 80000, Morocco.

We began with a single minibus and a vision: to offer cruise passengers a more authentic, personalized, and private-feeling experience of Morocco at prices that compete with large-group cruise line tours.

Today, our fleet of modern, air-conditioned minibuses and licensed local driver-guides welcome travelers from all over the world. We structure every itinerary to match cruise schedules, ensuring zero stress and a guaranteed on-time return.

Licensed Transport & Passenger Insurance

We are a fully registered operator complying with Moroccan transport ministry regulations. Full passenger liability insurance is included in every booking.

English, French & German Speaking Guides

Our professional local guides are fluent in multiple languages, sharing local history, secrets, and culture with clear, friendly explanations.
